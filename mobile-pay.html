Important Notes

Transaction Fees

Cross-network transfers usually have higher fees than same-network transfers. Check with your provider for current rates.

Security

Never share your mobile money PIN with anyone. Always verify the recipient's number before sending money.

Confirmation

Always wait for the SMS confirmation message before considering a transaction complete. Save this message as proof of payment.

Number Format

Ensure you enter the recipient's number in the correct format (07XX XXX XXX). The recipient must be registered with M-Pesa.

Processing Time

Cross-network transfers may take a few minutes to process. If the money doesn't arrive within 30 minutes, contact your provider.
